About

The PenParty competition at the Engineers' Spark event is a creative challenge where engineers design and build functional pens based on a given theme. Judged on functionality, innovation, and design, it's a highlight for participants to showcase their engineering and aesthetic skills in a competitive yet collaborative environment. Our mission is to raise awareness about cybersecurity by hosting various events, hands-on workshops and training sessions
