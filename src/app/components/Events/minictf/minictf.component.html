About

A Mini CTF (Capture The Flag) competition is a concise event designed for learning and practicing cybersecurity skills. Participants solve various challenges related to web security, cryptography, and forensics to find hidden "flags" and earn points. These competitions are excellent for developing and honing cybersecurity abilities in a competitive and educational setting.
