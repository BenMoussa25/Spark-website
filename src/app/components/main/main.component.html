Engineers Spark

About us

Engineers Spark is a community of IT enthusiasts spread across three universities: ISET'Com, FST and ESPRIT. Our club mainly revolves around ICT sciences, with a focus on cybersecurity, as well as web, mobile development and robotics . Our goal is to prepare students for better professional integration in the field. This is why we strive to offer quality training in the field of ICT. In addition we work on organizing multiple national events and projects in order for our members to apply their acquired skills.
